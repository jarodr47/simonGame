Press A Key or Click "Start Game" to Start

This Simon Says only shows one pattern event at a time, not the entire sequence. At the beginning of each level you will be shown one pattern event. This pattern event will be the last one you will click at the end of the sequence for that level.

For example say in level 1 you were shown "yellow", then to pass level one you click on the yellow block. Then on level two you are shown "red", to pass level two you click on yellow first, and then red. So on and so forth. Watch the video below for a visual demonstration.
